ADMISSION PROCESS

  • Parent/Guardian submit the online registration form. The applicable fee for the form is INR 500
  • For admission to Pre-KG – Grade 2, the Coordinator will meet the child for an interactive session.
  • For admission to Grade 3 and above, there will be a scholastic and skill-based assessment in literacy, numeracy, and science to check the readiness level of the child for the applied grade.
  • The child and the parent/guardian will meet the Principal for an interaction.
